Move-Out Responsibilities & Cleaning

At move-out, the apartment must be returned to a condition consistent with its state at move-in. This includes restoring cleanliness and removing all personal belongings.

Your Living Area

  • Clean the floor thoroughly
  • Wipe and clean the desk surface
  • Remove all personal belongings
  • Dispose of all trash before leaving

Kitchen (Common Area)

  • Clean the stove and kitchen island
  • Remove all personal food items from the refrigerator
  • Ensure the refrigerator door is fully closed

Final Checks Before Leaving

  • Ensure all three windows are closed
  • Ensure the air conditioning unit is turned off
  • Ensure the dryer filter is cleaned
  • Confirm all personal belongings have been removed
  • Ensure trash has been disposed of

Lock the Door

If you are required to return the key to someone other than my roommate, please ensure that the apartment door is properly locked using the key when you leave.

Further instructions regarding key return will be provided closer to your move-out date.

Failure to follow these may cause additional charge.

Daily Cleaning & Maintenance

Bathroom

  • Rinse away any hair left on the bathtub walls after showering
  • Keep the bathroom in a clean and usable condition

If the bathtub drain becomes clogged or drains slowly, please contact me directly. I will arrange building maintenance to resolve it quickly.

Kitchen

The kitchen is not frequently used by my roommate, so general maintenance should be sufficient rather than deep cleaning.

Cleaning Tools Available

  • A Swiffer mop is available in the bathroom
  • Additional mop sheets are available for use
  • You are welcome to use these tools for cleaning floors and surfaces
